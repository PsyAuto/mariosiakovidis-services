Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website. We do not collect any personal data using cookies. Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners in order to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, Marios Iakovidis Software Services) are called "first party cookies". Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "third party cookies". Third party cookies enable third party features or functionality to be provided on or through the website (e.g. like advertising, interactive content and analytics). The parties that set these third party cookies can recognize your computer both when it visits the website in question and also when it visits certain other websites.

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies

    • cookieBannerDisplayed

    The cookieBannerDisplayed cookie is used to improve the user experience on our website. It allows us to remember whether you have seen our cookie consent banner. This means that once you have made a choice about cookies, you won't be shown the banner again on subsequent visits.

    The cookieBannerDisplayed cookie also controls the display of Google Maps on our website. If you accept cookies, this cookie is set to true and Google Maps is enabled, allowing you to see a map on our website. If you decline cookies or have not made a choice yet, this cookie is not set or is set to false, and Google Maps is disabled to respect your privacy. This cookie does not store any personal data and is set to expire after one month.

  • External Cookies

    • Google Maps

    Our website uses Google Maps to display our work address. Google Maps is a service provided by Google Inc. When you use Google Maps on our website, Google collects data about your usage of the Maps service. This data may include your IP address and location. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how Google uses your data. We provide a cookie banner on our website that allows you to block this service if you choose to do so.
